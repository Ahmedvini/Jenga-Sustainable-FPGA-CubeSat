# SDG Alignment

## SDG 9: Industry, Innovation, and Infrastructure

PMEP supports modular and reusable satellite electronics interfaces.

## SDG 12: Responsible Consumption and Production

Duty cycling reduces energy use. Module replacement and reuse reduce electronic
waste compared with monolithic boards.

## SDG 17: Partnerships for the Goals

An open module interface can be shared between university teams, agencies, and
commercial CubeSat developers.
