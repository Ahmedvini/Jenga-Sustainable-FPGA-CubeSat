# Lifecycle Analysis

The modular architecture reduces lifecycle impact in three ways:

- A failed module can be replaced without discarding the whole electronics stack.
- Validated modules can be reused across missions.
- Lower power reduces battery stress and may extend useful mission lifetime.

The Phase 1 simulation quantifies operational energy savings. A full lifecycle
assessment would add manufacturing energy, launch mass impact, replacement
frequency, and end-of-life disposal assumptions.
